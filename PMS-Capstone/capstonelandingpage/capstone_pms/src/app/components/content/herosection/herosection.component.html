Leading Doctors

In hospitals, the practice of medicine is not just about treating illnesses and injuries, it's about caring for the whole person. This quote by the ancient Greek physician Hippocrates speaks to the idea that medicine is not just a science, but an art that requires compassion and empathy for those in need. It reminds us that the best hospitals are those where patients are not only treated with the most advanced medical techniques, but also with kindness and understanding.
